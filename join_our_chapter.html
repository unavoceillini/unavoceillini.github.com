How to Join

We are currently not officially taking members as we are not yet formally recognized by Una Voce America, but we are seeking people who are interested in our cause and supporting us. If you have questions about membership not answered on this page please email us at unavoceillini@gmail.com

To join our cause we ask that you join our mailing list, and attend mass with us, as often as you can, as a group on the first Saturday of the month. The location and time for first Saturday masses will be announced on the mailing list and front page of this website. Details on how to join the mailing list are below. In addition we ask that you offer up masses and rosaries for the intentions of our chapter, and pray for the intersession of our patrons Our Lady of Perpetual Help, Saint Gregory the Great, and Saint Pius V.

Mailing List

To subscribe to our mailing list you can either type your email address in the Subscribe to our mailing list! box near the bottom of the left sidebar and then press the Go button, this should bring up a pop up window to Google Groups were you can finish the process of subscribing.

The other way to subscribe to our mailing list is to send an empty email to unavoceillini+subscribe@googlegroups.com

We use our mailing list to send out mass times for our groups first Saturday devotion, requests for petitions, and news about other special events of interest to our members. If you use facebook please join our Facebook group!
