Una Voce Illini is an official chapter of Una Voce America in Champaign-Urbana. We are a group of faithful Roman Catholics who share the goal of promoting and facilitating the celebration of the extraordinary form of the Mass (also known as the Traditional Latin Mass or the Tridentine Latin Mass). We also support and promote related traditional practices, such as Gregorian chant.

May 9 - Traditional Latin Mass at St. Elizabeth

Father Bird will be celebrating his first Missa Cantata (sung high mass) at 7:00 PM at St. Elizabeth's in Thomasboro, IL on Ascension Thursday. The Mass will feature a full choir, torchbearers, and incense!

The choir will perform William Byrd's Mass for Five Voices as well as plainchant and Byrd's polyphonic settings of the Propers. The organist will also play early English organ works by Byrd, John Redford, and Thomas Preston. The choir will consist of five professional singers: Yaritza Zayas and Jihyun Kim, soprano, Chris Holman, countertenor, Stephen Boyer, tenor, and John Wagstaff, baritone.

Una Voce Illini will make a brief announcement prior to Mass about the aims of Una Voce. After Mass we will be signing up members. This is a very important event for Una Voce Illini and for building a Latin Mass Community in the Champaign area so please invite all your friends!
