Latin Mass Directory

The Coalition in support of Ecclesia Dei compiles an online and print directory of traditional Latin Masses offered regularly in U.S. dioceses. Their directory is one of the most comprehensive and up-to-date resources that we have found.

You can check their directory here for the latest information. (last updated March 12, 2013).

There are currently five parishes in the diocese of Peoria that offer the traditional Latin Mass. Three of these parishes offer the traditional Mass weekly on Sunday.
